## What is Nada?

Nada is a powerful native iOS application that transforms your voice into a musical instrument. Powered by our custom **Tune Lab Engine**, it bridges the gap between fleeting vocal ideas and digital music production. Whether you are humming a melody, singing a bassline, or beatboxing a drum loop, Nada converts your audio into multi-track MIDI compositions in real-time.

### Key Features

*   **Voice to Melody:** Hum, whistle, or sing to play pianos, synths, guitars, and basses. Our real-time pitch detection and smart quantization ensure you always hit the right notes in your chosen Key and Scale.
*   **Voice to Beats:** Turn beatboxing into tight drum loops. Use the **Beat Mode** to train the engine to recognize your specific sounds‚Äî"Boom" becomes a Kick, "Kak" becomes a Snare.
*   **Multi-Track Studio:** Layer your ideas into a full song. Arrange, mute, mix, and tune multiple tracks using the timeline view.
*   **SoundFont Support:** Comes with high-quality built-in sounds, but is fully compatible with standard `.sf2` SoundFonts, giving you infinite instrument possibilities.
*   **Privacy First:** All audio processing happens locally on your device. Your voice recordings and musical ideas are yours and are never uploaded to the cloud.
*   **Export & Share:** Export your creations as standard MIDI files (`.mid`) to use in Logic Pro, Ableton, or GarageBand, or share raw audio renders (`.m4a`).

### Bug Report Guidelines

A great bug report is detailed and reproducible. Please include:

*   **A clear title:** e.g., "Pitch detection latency increases after recording 3 tracks."
*   **Steps to reproduce:** Exactly what you did that caused the bug.
*   **What you expected to happen:** e.g., "I expected the recording to stop immediately."
*   **What actually happened:** e.g., "The app continued recording for 2 seconds before stopping."
*   **Device Information:** The model of your iPhone (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro) and the iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.1).
*   **Screenshots or Screen Recordings:** These are incredibly helpful, especially for UI glitches or specific engine settings!

### Feature Request Guidelines

We love hearing your ideas! To make your suggestion clear, please include:

*   **A clear title:** e.g., "Add AUv3 support to use Nada as a plugin."
*   **The problem you're trying to solve:** Why is this feature needed? e.g., "I want to use my own synthesizer apps while controlling them with my voice."
*   **Your proposed solution:** A brief description of how you imagine the feature working.
